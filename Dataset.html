  • SHMetro Dataset: a large-scale dataset for metro ridership prediction [Download]

    • 288 metro stations in Shanghai, China.
    • 811.8 million transaction records (7/01/2016-9/31/2016).
    • For each station, the inflow and outflow of every 15 minutes are measured.

    Citation: “Physical-Virtual Collaboration Graph Network for Station-Level Metro Ridership Prediction”, Jingwen Chen and Lingbo Liu and Hefeng Wu and Jiajie Zhen and Guanbin Li and Liang Lin.
